Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 17, 2026

1. Introduction

Welcome to Reely. We respect your privacy and are committed to protecting your personal data. This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect the following types of information:

  • Account Information: When you sign up using Google, we receive your name, email address, and profile picture.
  • Uploaded Content: Screenshots you upload to our service for location analysis.
  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with our service.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide and maintain our service
  • Process your uploaded screenshots using AI to extract location information
  • Store your upload history for your convenience
  • Improve and personalize your experience
  • Communicate with you about service updates

4. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services:

  • Google Authentication: For secure sign-in
  • Google Gemini AI: To analyze screenshots and extract location data
  • Google Maps: To provide location links and information
  • Supabase: For user authentication and data storage
  • MongoDB: For storing upload history

5. Data Security

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is 100% secure, and we cannot guarantee absolute security.

6. Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active or as needed to provide you services. You can request deletion of your data at any time.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Request deletion of your data
  • Export your data

8. Contact Us

If you have questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@reely.app
